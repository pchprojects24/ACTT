i-STAT Handheld Blood Analyzer

Portable point-of-care analyzer for rapid blood chemistry, blood gas, cardiac markers, and coagulation testing in the Sick Bay. Results available in 2–3 minutes with no laboratory infrastructure required.

Quick Overview

  • Handheld analyzer using single-use cartridges filled with 2–3 drops of whole blood.
  • Results in 2–3 minutes; no laboratory infrastructure required.
  • Operated by trained Sick Bay personnel; requires QC verification before patient testing.
  • Store analyzer at room temperature; cartridges must be warmed from refrigerator before use.

Key specifications

Sample typeWhole blood (capillary or venous); 2–3 drops per cartridge
Result time2–3 minutes per cartridge
ConnectivityDownload to RALS-Plus or equivalent LIS/HIS via cradle/wireless
QC requirementElectronic QC simulator (E3) before patient testing; per-shift or after transport
Cartridge storageRefrigerated 2–8°C; warm to room temperature before use (5 min)
Analyzer storageRoom temperature; avoid humidity/extremes; carry case provided

Available Cartridges

  • CHEM8+ — electrolytes (Na, K, Cl), BUN/Cr, glucose, ionized Ca, Hct/Hgb
  • CG4+ — blood gas (pH, pCO₂, pO₂), lactate, base excess
  • PT/INR — coagulation/anticoagulation status
  • Troponin I — cardiac biomarker for ACS evaluation

Device Care

  • Store analyzer at room temperature; avoid extreme heat, cold, or moisture.
  • Cartridges refrigerated at 2–8°C; warm to room temperature for at least 5 minutes before use.
  • Run electronic QC simulator before each shift and after transport to verify analyzer function.
  • Clean analyzer surface with mild disinfectant wipe; avoid liquid ingress into ports.
  • Inspect the cartridge port and connector pins routinely; remove any visible contamination with a dry swab.
