Monitoring & Diagnostics
i-STAT Blood Analyzer
Portable point-of-care analyzer — chemistry (CHEM8+), blood gas (CG4+), coagulation (PT/INR), and cardiac biomarkers (Troponin I). Results in 2–3 min.

Zoll Propaq MD Monitor/Defibrillator
Portable monitor/defibrillator with 12-lead ECG, SpO₂, NIBP, EtCO₂, manual defibrillation, AED, cardioversion, external pacing, and Real CPR Help®. Official ZOLL training videos available.

Zoll EMV+ 731 Ventilator
Portable turbine ventilator for adult, pediatric, and infant patients. Modes: A/C-VC, A/C-PC, SIMV, CPAP, NIV. Runs on internal battery (~10 h), AC, or vehicle power. Operates without external O₂ supply.

B. Braun Infusomat Space

Supportive Care
Oxygen — Invacare Platinum 10
Stationary 10 L/min oxygen concentrator. Produces medical-grade O₂ from room air — no cylinder refills during normal operation. Requires uninterrupted AC power; cylinder backup essential.
Specs Videos Nav
Suction — Laerdal LSU
Portable rechargeable suction unit. Up to 530 mmHg vacuum; adjustable for adult/pediatric/neonatal use. Operates on battery, AC, or vehicle power. Videos available.

Cocoon Warming Device
Active forced-air patient warming for hypothermia prevention and treatment. Used post-arrest, post-trauma, and exposure casualties. Monitor temperature and skin every 15–30 min.

Belmont buddy lite Fluid Warmer
Portable battery-powered in-line fluid warmer. Warms crystalloid at up to 4.8 L/hr and packed RBCs at up to 50 mL/min to physiological temperature. Official full instructional video available.

Incubator — Millipore XX631
Single-chamber portable incubator for field microbiology (membrane filtration). Maintains 35–37°C (coliforms) or 44.5°C (thermotolerant tests). Foam-lined carry case for field use.
