Incubator — Millipore XX631

Single-chamber portable incubator (XX631K series) for field and laboratory microbiology work, primarily membrane filtration for water quality testing. Maintains precise temperature for bacterial culture plates.

Quick Start Key Specs Functions Troubleshooting Care References

Quick start

  1. Place the incubator on a level surface with vents unobstructed; connect to AC (or approved DC supply if available).
  2. Set the thermostat to the target temperature (e.g., 35–37 °C for coliforms, 44.5 °C for thermotolerant tests) and close the lid.
  3. Allow the chamber to warm and stabilize; verify using the built-in thermometer before loading samples.
  4. Load sealed plates/tubes without overcrowding; recheck the thermometer periodically during incubation.
  5. Record start/stop times and temperatures; remove samples promptly at the end of the run.

Common functions

  • Fine-tune the thermostat knob to maintain set temperature; small adjustments can prevent overshoot.
  • Use the viewing window/thermometer to monitor temperature without prolonged lid opening.
  • Transport using the carry case; allow a few minutes to re-stabilize after moving.

Troubleshooting

  • Not heating: Confirm power, check fuse, and raise the thermostat above ambient; ensure the indicator light is on.
  • Temperature unstable/overshoot: Reduce the setpoint slightly and allow time to stabilize; check that the lid gasket is seated and vents are clear.
  • Condensation: Briefly crack the lid or dry the chamber between runs; avoid wet plates contacting the heater surface.

Care & consumables

  • Wipe interior/exterior with mild detergent after use; avoid harsh solvents on seals or acrylic surfaces.
  • Periodically verify thermometer accuracy with a calibrated reference.
  • Store dry with the lid ajar to prevent odor and mildew.
