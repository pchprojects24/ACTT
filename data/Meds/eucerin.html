Eucerin

Stocked on board

  • Eucerin 1kg Cream

Adult dosing

  • Dry skin / xerosis
    • Apply liberally to affected areas as needed, typically 1–3 times daily or more frequently
  • Atopic dermatitis / eczema (adjunctive moisturizer)
    • Apply liberally to affected areas 2–3 times daily, or after bathing/hand washing
    • Use as part of comprehensive management (along with prescribed topical therapies when indicated)
  • Minor skin irritation / barrier support
    • Apply to affected areas as needed to maintain skin barrier integrity

Indications

  • Dry skin / xerosis (environmental, aging, occupation-related)
  • Supportive care in atopic dermatitis, eczema, psoriasis (adjunct to other therapies)
  • Prevention and management of minor skin irritation and chapping
  • Barrier protection for hands in frequent-washing environments

Contraindications / cautions

  • Known hypersensitivity to any component of the formulation
  • Avoid application to open wounds or severely damaged/infected skin unless directed
  • Not for ophthalmic use
  • If rash or irritation develops after application, discontinue use

Adverse effects

  • Generally very well tolerated
  • Rare: contact dermatitis or irritation in sensitive individuals
  • Greasy feel (formulation-dependent; may affect acceptability/adherence)

Preparation & administration

  • Apply topically to clean, dry skin
  • Most effective when applied to slightly damp skin (e.g., immediately after bathing) to lock in moisture
  • Gentle massage until absorbed; allow time before dressing if applying to large areas
  • Store at room temperature; avoid contamination of bulk container

Monitoring

  • Skin condition improvement (hydration, reduced scaling/cracking)
  • Signs of irritation or allergic reaction
  • Adherence/tolerability (greasy feel may reduce compliance in some users)

At-sea considerations

  • Useful for prevention: encourage regular use in dry/harsh environmental conditions (wind, salt water, cold)
  • Large container allows for liberal use; consider individual dispensing to minimize contamination
  • Effective barrier support for hands with frequent washing/glove use
  • Non-medicated option: safe for extended use without systemic concerns
