Weekly Checks

Sustainment tasks to keep the analyzer reliable during deployment.

Weekly routine

  • Deep clean exterior, keys, and dock cradle with 70% IPA; ensure cartridge port is dry and free of salt or debris.
  • Inspect dock power leads and printer paper; verify analyzer charges normally when seated.
  • Review inventory: rotate refrigerated cartridges (FIFO), discard expired/damaged pouches, and verify out-of-fridge labels.
  • Run the electronic simulator and at least one level of liquid QC for any cartridge lot that has not been QC’d in the past week (or per local QC plan); record results.
  • Verify date/time, operator list, and connectivity/printing; export or document stored results, then clear space if needed.
  • Check spare battery (if held) completes a full charge cycle; replace if run time is poor.

After transport or rough seas

  • Re-run the electronic simulator before patient testing.
  • Inspect cartridges for condensation; discard any with compromised seals.
