Pregnancy Test

Rapid hCG testing references for onboard pregnancy screening.

Available Tests

  • OSOM hCG Combo Test — detects human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG), manufacturer: Sekisui Diagnostics.
  • QuickVue hCG Urine Test — detects human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG), manufacturer: Quidel.

Sample Type

  • OSOM hCG Combo Test: Urine or serum.
  • QuickVue hCG Urine Test: Urine only.

When to Use

  • Pregnancy screening prior to medications or procedures where pregnancy status affects care decisions.
  • Evaluation of amenorrhea, nausea/vomiting, pelvic pain, or other symptoms where pregnancy is in the differential.

Operational Workflow

  1. Confirm patient identity and document test indication.
  2. Select the appropriate kit based on available sample type (urine or serum).
  3. Run and interpret test strictly per the manufacturer kit insert.
  4. Record product name, lot number, sample type, and result in the medical record.
  5. If result is unexpected or inconsistent with presentation, repeat per protocol or send confirmatory testing when available.

Interpretation Reminders

  • A positive result indicates hCG is detected and should be correlated with history and clinical findings.
  • A negative result does not always exclude very early pregnancy; repeat testing may be needed if suspicion remains.
  • Follow shipboard and command policy for documentation, counseling, and follow-up planning.
