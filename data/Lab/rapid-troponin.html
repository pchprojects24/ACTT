Rapid Troponin

Rapid point-of-care troponin workflow for chest pain triage when lab support is limited.

Purpose

  • Provide fast assessment for myocardial injury in suspected ACS.
  • Support evacuation and treatment decisions when timelines are compressed at sea.

Workflow Summary

  1. Obtain baseline ECG and clinical assessment before or during sample collection.
  2. Collect sample using approved i-STAT troponin cartridge workflow.
  3. Run initial test as time zero (T0), document time precisely.
  4. Repeat troponin at interval per protocol (commonly ~3 hours) if concern remains.
  5. Trend symptoms, ECG changes, and serial troponin results together.

Operational Notes

  • A single negative early troponin does not exclude ACS.
  • Interpretation must be combined with history, exam, and ECG findings.
  • Use the same analyzer workflow and documentation standards each run to reduce errors.

Documentation Checklist

  • Symptom onset time and chest pain characteristics.
  • Troponin result time stamps (T0 and repeat).
  • ECG interpretation and any interval changes.
  • Disposition decision (observe, treat onboard, or evacuate).
