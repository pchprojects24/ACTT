Rapid Strep

Rapid detection of Group A Streptococcus for sore throat evaluation.

Available Test

  • QuickVue In-Line Strep A Test — rapid test to detect Group A Streptococcus (Strep A), manufacturer: Quidel.

Clinical Use

  • Helps identify strep throat / Strep A infection in patients with compatible symptoms.
  • Supports treatment planning and return-to-duty counseling when strep infection is suspected.

Operational Workflow

  1. Collect throat specimen per manufacturer instructions.
  2. Run the QuickVue In-Line Strep A Test exactly as directed in kit instructions.
  3. Read result at the instructed time window only.
  4. Document result, product used, and lot number in the medical record.

Interpretation Reminders

  • Positive result supports Group A Strep infection in the appropriate clinical setting.
  • Negative rapid testing may still need follow-on clinical reassessment if suspicion remains high.
  • Correlate results with symptom severity, exam findings, and local treatment guidance.
