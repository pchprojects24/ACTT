Monospot

Rapid heterophile antibody screening for infectious mononucleosis (IM).

Available Test

  • Clearview IM II — rapid test kit for infectious mononucleosis (mono), manufacturer: Abbott.

Clinical Use

  • Helps identify infectious mononucleosis in patients with compatible symptoms.
  • Useful when evaluating persistent pharyngitis, fatigue, cervical adenopathy, or mono-like illness.

Operational Workflow

  1. Confirm test indication and patient identity.
  2. Collect and process specimen exactly per the Clearview IM II package insert.
  3. Interpret only within the defined read window.
  4. Document product name, lot, and result in the encounter note.

Interpretation Reminders

  • Positive result supports IM in the correct clinical context.
  • A negative result does not fully exclude early illness; reassess clinically if symptoms persist.
  • Pair test results with exam findings and duty/activity counseling based on command policy.
